    About Us

    Amstor Technologies LLC is one of the best health care information management system providers for polyclinic and medical centers all across UAE and GCC countries. Amstor is a fast-growing software development company headquartered in Dubai, UAE with a state-of-the art software Development centre based in Trivandrum Techno Park providing cutting-edge web-based clinic management system (EMR) under the brand name of Amstor LeHealth. LeHealth is a specially designed application to serve the end-to-end operational requirements of small, medium and large level of polyclinics, medical centers and specialty clinics in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, UAE and GCC countries. It provides an easy and anywhere access to the end users and guarantees the smooth transactions & great level of patient satisfaction. Amstor Technologies LLC is a part of Amstor Group of companies.

    LeHealth Services

    “Amstor LeHealth” offers a highly customizable EMR solution with advanced integration facility to provide seamless integrations with Patient Web Portal, 3rd party accounting management/ERP system, Lab machine interfacing and PACS systems.

    Along with long serving history in providing sophisticated EMR solutions for healthcare industry, we also extend our services for making it more advanced and integrated to work as a one stop solution to provide Up-To-Date and accurate information. Our services are as follows;

    Advanced Patient Web-portal

    “Amstor” offers an advanced fully customized Patient Web Portal for easy patient access for Online-appointment booking, Payment and Result access.

    Patient portal, which offers the patient to access the clinic specialty details and doctor availability with available slots for advance appointment booking. The portal will be linked with clinic website, so the patients can access the portal from anywhere any time without any hassle. We offer fully customized patient portal to provide best fitting solution based on the clinic’s requirements. Also, as an extended services, clinics can give options for online payments, patient profile update, Result and medical report.etc.

    Product Customization

    LeHealth Software extends immense capability and flexibility to scaleup the product features based on the regional requirements and customer specific requirements to match the specific workflow and business rules, thus it provides greater flexibility, easy to use, accurate and up-to-date data at any time analysis.

    PACS system Integration.

    Lehealth offers a comprehensive integrated Radiology information management Module which is capable to integrate with the PACS system to avail the image access to the doctors. This allows the doctors to see the image while patient case sheet is open.

    Integration with Standard Accounting/ERP Software Integration

    LeHealth Software offers the capability to integrate the software with any global standard accounting software or ERP system to achieve the data sharing to make the booking keeping Up to Date. The integrations are done through the API integrations or DB integrations. The seamless integrations can facilitate whole solutions to work as single platform to produce desired result faster and accurate.

    LIMS – LAB Machine Interfacing

    Lab Machine interfacing is a must to have facility for all medical laboratories to make the testing and publishing the result easy and accurate. Lehealth Lab management module provides the facility to interface with lab machines Unidirectional or bi-Directional to generate the results based on the equipment capacity.
