    About Our Company

    We 're delivering the best
    customer experience

    Amstor Technologies LLC is one of the best health care information management system providers for polyclinic and medical centers all across UAE and GCC countries. Amstor is a fast-growing software development company headquartered in Dubai, UAE with a state-of-the art software Development centre based in Trivandrum Techno Park providing cutting-edge web-based clinic management system (EMR) under the brand name of Amstor LeHealth. LeHealth is a specially designed application to serve the end-to-end operational requirements of small, medium and large level of polyclinics, medical centers and specialty clinics in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, UAE and GCC countries. It provides an easy and anywhere access to the end users and guarantees the smooth transactions & great level of patient satisfaction. Amstor Technologies LLC is a part of Amstor Group of companies.
